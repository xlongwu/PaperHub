More significantly, **"vectorless RAG"** ([PageIndex](https://github.com/VectifyAI/PageIndex)) and **tokenizer-free TTS** ([VoxCPM](https://github.com/OpenBMB/VoxCPM)) represent architectural bets against embedding-heavy and tokenization-bound paradigms.
