**Agent memory and methodology are the two hottest problem spaces.** [claude-mem](https://github.com/thedotmack/claude-mem) attacks session continuity through automated capture and compression, while [superpowers](https://github.com/obra/superpowers) proposes an entire software development methodology for agentic work. Meanwhile, TypeScript dominates the agent/application layer, reflecting the web-developer demographic's central role in AI tooling creation.

**RAG and agent boundaries have dissolved.** Projects like [ragflow](https://github.com/infiniflow/ragflow) and [PageIndex](https://github.com/VectifyAI/PageIndex) no longer market themselves as pure retrieval systems but as reasoning-augmented agent context layers. The vector database category is simultaneously being challenged by vectorless approaches and absorbed into broader agent memory architectures.
