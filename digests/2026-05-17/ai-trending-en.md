## 3. Trend Signal Analysis

**Agent Infrastructure > Model Development**: The most explosive signal is the complete inversion of developer attention. Where 2023-2024 saw raw LLM training repos dominate, May 2026 shows **agent harnesses, skill frameworks, and orchestration layers** capturing 70%+ of new stars. [superpowers](https://github.com/obra/superpowers) and [scientific-agent-skills](https://github.com/K-Dense-AI/scientific-agent-skills) aren't building models—they're building **capabilities that make models useful**. This reflects market maturation: base models are commoditized (accessible via Ollama, APIs), but agent composition remains unsolved.

**Rust as AI Infrastructure Language**: Four of eight trending repos use Rust ([bun](https://github.com/oven-sh/bun), [openhuman](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/openhuman), [RuView](https://github.com/ruvnet/RuView), [googleworkspace/cli](https://github.com/googleworkspace/cli)). This isn't coincidence—Rust's memory safety and performance are critical for **on-device AI** (supertonic, openhuman) and **real-time spatial processing** (RuView). The "private, local, fast" triad demands systems-level languages.

**Ambient Intelligence Emerges**: [RuView](https://github.com/ruvnet/RuView)'s WiFi-based spatial intelligence represents a new category—**sensorless perception** for agents. No cameras, no wearables; commodity hardware becomes context source. This pairs with [supertonic](https://github.com/supertone-inc/supertonic)'s on-device TTS to form a **private ambient AI stack**: perceive without exposing, respond without cloud dependency.

**"No Content Filters" as Differentiator**: [Open-Generative-AI](https://github.com/Anil-matcha/Open-Generative-AI)'s explicit "no content filters, self-hosted" positioning reflects regulatory/privacy backlash against centralized AI. The MIT license + self-hosting combination is becoming a competitive weapon.

**Claude Code Ecosystem Consolidation**: Multiple repos ([codegraph](https://github.com/colbymchenry/codegraph), [ruflo](https://github.com/ruvnet/ruflo), [claude-mem](https://github.com/thedotmack/claude-mem), [everything-claude-code](https://github.com/affaan-m/everything-claude-code)) explicitly target Claude Code integration. Anthropic's CLI tool has become a **platform** with its own extension economy, similar to VS Code's emergence.
