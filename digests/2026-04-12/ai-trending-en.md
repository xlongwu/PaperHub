## 3. Trend Signal Analysis

**Explosive Attention: Agent Determinism Infrastructure**

The pain point is **non-determinism**—Claude Code, Cursor, and similar tools produce inconsistent results across sessions, blocking production deployment. Open-source is responding with "harness builders," skill frameworks, and managed agent platforms that promise repeatable, observable behavior.

This indicates MCP is becoming the "USB-C for AI tools"—universal adapter layer between agents and capabilities.

[Kronos](https://github.com/shiyu-coder/Kronos) represents a notable vertical expansion—foundation models specialized for financial markets language, distinct from general-purpose LLMs. This follows the pattern of legal (Harvey), medical (OpenEvidence), and coding (Cursor) verticalization, suggesting domain-specific "language of X" models as a viable open-source category.
