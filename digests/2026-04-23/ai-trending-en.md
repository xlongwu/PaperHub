**Security AI Goes Offensive.** `Shannon`'s white-box pentesting approach—autonomous source analysis plus real exploit execution—marks a threshold crossing. Previous security AI was defensive (SAST, dependency scanning). Shannon is *offensive*, creating autonomous red-team capabilities. **"Vectorless RAG" Challenges Embedding Orthodoxy.** `PageIndex` (25,646 stars) and `LEANN` (97% storage savings) collectively question whether dense vector retrieval is the inevitable RAG architecture. With reasoning-based indexing and learned nearest-neighbor methods, we're seeing early signals of post-vector-search retrieval—critical as embedding storage costs scale with multimodal data.

**China's AI Open Source Ascendancy.** Multiple trending projects (`TrendRadar`, `Pixelle-Video`, `CowAgent`, `minimind`, `zvec`, `OpenSandbox`) originate from Chinese developers or target Chinese platforms (WeChat, Lark, DingTalk). Ollama's explicit support for GLM-5, MiniMax, Kimi-K2.5 confirms Chinese foundation models are achieving global toolchain integration. This geographic diversification reduces OpenAI/Anthropic centrality.

**WiFi as Computer Vision Alternative.** `RuView`'s WiFi DensePose—pose estimation without cameras—opens novel privacy-preserving sensing for agents and robotics. The 565 daily stars indicate interest in *non-visual* perception modalities as computer vision faces regulatory and privacy friction.
